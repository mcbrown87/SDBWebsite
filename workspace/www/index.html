Bio

"Old school joints for the true funk soldiers..."

Shut Down Brown bridges the gap between classic 90's R&B and its predecessor, 70's soul. They are a live funk band with big horns and tons of energy. Rarely is there a show played where the funk-step isn't organically busted out by the band, the crowd or both. They thrive on the raunchy grooves that make ya move. They take after The Greats with copious improvisation and on-stage communication. "Big time, Good time" is their all-in-one motto, mission and mentality.
